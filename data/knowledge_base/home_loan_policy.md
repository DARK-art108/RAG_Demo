# Home Loan Policy

## Eligibility
- Minimum age: 21 years, maximum age at loan maturity: 65 years.
- Salaried applicants need at least 2 years of total work experience.
- Self-employed applicants need at least 3 years of business continuity.

## Loan Terms
- Loan tenure can range from 5 to 30 years.
- Floating interest rate starts from 8.65% per annum.
- Processing fee is up to 0.5% of sanctioned amount, capped at INR 10,000.

## Documents Required
- Identity proof, address proof, and PAN.
- Last 6 months salary slips or last 2 years ITR for self-employed.
- Property documents and sale agreement.
