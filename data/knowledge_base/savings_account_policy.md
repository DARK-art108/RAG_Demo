# Savings Account Policy

## Account Opening
- Resident individuals can open account with valid KYC.
- Minor account can be opened with guardian details.
- Video KYC is available for digital onboarding in supported locations.

## Minimum Balance
- Urban branches: INR 10,000 average monthly balance.
- Semi-urban branches: INR 5,000 average monthly balance.
- Rural branches: INR 2,500 average monthly balance.

## Service Charges
- Non-maintenance charges apply when monthly average balance falls short.
- Debit card annual fee may apply after first year based on account variant.
- NEFT and RTGS charges follow current bank schedule and regulations.
