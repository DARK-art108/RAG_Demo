# Credit Card Policy

## Eligibility
- Applicant must be 18 years or older.
- Minimum monthly income requirement: INR 25,000 for standard cards.
- Existing customer relationship can improve approval speed.

## Fees
- Annual fee varies by card variant from INR 0 to INR 2,999.
- Late payment fee is based on statement outstanding slabs.
- Cash withdrawal fee is 2.5% of withdrawn amount with minimum INR 500.

## Interest and Billing
- Interest-free period is up to 45 days if full dues are paid.
- Revolving credit attracts monthly interest as per card terms.
- Minimum due payment avoids immediate default but accrues interest.
